Schroders plc has announced transactions by persons discharging managerial responsibilities involving the acquisition of ordinary shares under a dividend re-investment plan. Specifically, Richard Oldfield, Meagen Burnett, Johanna Kyrklund, and Georg Wunderlin acquired a total of 437 ordinary shares at a price of £5.79 per share on 23 April 2026, conducted on the London Stock Exchange.
